🤖 Should AI Replace Human Teachers?

Artificial intelligence is changing the way we learn. AI tutors can answer questions instantly, adapt to each student's learning speed, and are available 24 hours a day. Some schools are already using AI programs to help students learn math and reading.

Supporters say AI could make education more equal. Students in rural areas or poor neighborhoods often don't have access to the same quality teachers as students in wealthy cities. AI could give every student access to the same high-quality education.

However, many educators argue that teaching is not just about sharing information. Teachers build emotional connections with students, notice when a child is struggling emotionally, and inspire students in ways that machines cannot. A good teacher doesn't just teach — they mentor, encourage, and care.

South Korea has already introduced AI digital textbooks in English classes starting in 2025. While many parents support this change, some teachers worry about losing jobs and human connection in classrooms.

What do you think — should AI replace human teachers, or should it just support them?

AI GUIDE
찬성(AI가 선생님을 대체해야 한다) 또는 반대(AI는 보조 도구여야 한다) 중 내 입장을 먼저 고르세요. 그 다음 논거를 3가지 정리해봅니다.
✅ 찬성 (FOR) AI가 선생님을 대체해야 한다
❌ 반대 (AGAINST) AI는 보조 도구여야 한다
✅ 찬성 논거 (FOR)
  • 24시간 즉각 답변 — 언제든 질문 가능
  • 학생별 학습 속도에 자동 맞춤
  • 지방·저소득층 교육 불평등 해소
  • 감정에 치우치지 않는 공정한 평가
❌ 반대 논거 (AGAINST)
  • 감정 연결 & 정서적 지원 불가
  • 아이의 어려움을 맥락으로 파악 못함
  • 영감 & 동기 부여는 사람만 가능
  • 예상치 못한 상황에 유연하게 대처 어려움
