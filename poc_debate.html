KEY ISSUE & DEBATE · L2
🦖
Cloning Dinosaurs
Find the Answer
What recent movie made people think about dinosaurs?

Millions of people around the world headed to theaters in June to watch the new sci-fi film "Jurassic World." They saw what can happen when scientists attempt to bring dinosaurs back to life. The movie made many people wonder if scientists should ever try to clone dinosaurs.

Currently, it is impossible for scientists to clone dinosaurs. Scientists have never been able to find any dinosaur DNA. In the future, however, scientists could make new discoveries.

Some people think that scientists should try to clone dinosaurs, arguing it would help them learn much more about these fascinating creatures and excite ordinary people enough to donate to scientific projects.

On the other hand, many people worry dinosaurs could not be controlled, and that the project would be far too expensive compared to other important research priorities.
